Log in to Gerrit

We are using Gerrit for code reviewing so first you need to configure your Gerrit account. Our Gerrit is doing authentication based on OpenID. This means you will need to have an account with an OpenID provider like Google or Yahoo. After you signed up you need to pick a user name, add your email addresses and SSH keys. The first time you log in all this can be done from the landing page.


  1. Navigate to Gerrit

    Go to http://saros-build.imp.fu-berlin.de/gerrit and press Sign In (top right corner).

    If your browser fails to display the website or you get an error just try http://saros-build.imp.fu-berlin.de/gerrit?s=0 to disable server side scripting.

  2. Select an OpenID Provider

    Temporary there is a problem in our Gerrit system so that you only can use Google accounts.

    Select your OpenID provider, e.g. Yahoo, Google, Livejournal, MyOpenID.
    If you don't have an OpenID account, go for it!

  3. Sign into your OpenID Provider

    You may need to sign into your account.

  4. Authorize the usage by Gerrit

    Allow to use your identity by Gerrit.

  5. Configure the Gerrit account

    Your browser will return to our Gerrit start page. Depending on your OpenID provider your full name and email address maybe are already configured.

    1. fill in Username: Your desired user name and click Select Username

      Known issue: Internal Server Error

      If you get an Internal Server Error maybe your username is already in use. Choose a different one.

    2. (Not needed if you use Google:) click Register New Email ...

    3. (Not needed if you use Google:) fill in your email address and click Register

    4. (Not needed if you use Google:) check your mailbox for new messages and confirm your email address by clicking the link in the mail

    5. (Not needed if you use Google:) fill in Full Name: Your real name and click Save Changes

Now your Gerrit account almost configured. In the next two chapters we will configure your Eclipse to interact with Gerrit.
